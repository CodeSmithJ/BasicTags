What is a "gun bunny"...?

A nickname for an artilleryman of course!

They call us gun bunnies because we move around the howitzer quick as lightning performing the tasks that follow.

  1. Open breach
  2. Load the round
  3. Close breach
  4. Pull the string
  5. Go BOOM!

"Allegedly" that's all we do however they don't call us the king of battle for no reason.

Somebody pulled the string!
